About

Adam is a Chicago-based junior developer. He has an Information Systems BA with honors and years of experience as a webmaster, office manager and communications coordinator. Adam has a passion for coding, learning, visual design, and helping others.

He also likes audiobooks, morning meditation, finding quiet along the lakefront, exploring and learning with his wife and spending low-key time with family. He lives in the South Loop.

Adam is currently available for junior front end developer jobs, apprenticeships and possibly internships.
